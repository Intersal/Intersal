Why Love?

Love is something that I think is the most important thing in life. I think that if everyone loved each other, the world would be a much better place. I think that love is the most powerful force in the universe. I think that love is what makes life worth living. I think that love is what makes us human.

And I know, of course Love is what makes us human and that I'm only stating the obvious. But, I dont see many people who actually believe it. Sometimes the most obvious and overdone lessons are the least used.
